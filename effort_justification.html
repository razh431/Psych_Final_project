Effort Justification

9 May 2021

Time really flew past this semester. Looking back, I was struggling to catch up with never-ending deadlines, juggling sleep, grades, and time to relax, and buckling under the pressure repeatedly. I seemed to put in so much effort in this semester with mediocre everything, at best. The effort just didn’t seem to justify the effort that I put in this semester to do everything I can.

I guess the only thing I feel guilty about is spending my parents’ money for tuition. We’re not financially stable, but they’ve supported my decision to come here.

In retrospect, I guess it’s not that I didn’t enjoy my time at Cornell. I’ve learned a lot, grown a lot, and I appreciate the changes and obstacles. I am aware that this change of cognition may be my effort of justifying this Covid semester, but I think I look forward to a new semester here.

Rachel
