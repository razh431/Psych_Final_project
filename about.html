About This Project

This website is created for the Final Project for Psych 2800, Spring 2021 semester. Through this project, I attempt to find examples of how social psych could affect young adults on a college campus by telling the story through a blog entry.

Through the blog entries, I try to reflect on certain parts of the lives of people around me, although some blog posts are certainly the creation of my imagination. Through this website, I try to synthesize different topics of psychology covered this semester by identifying how they could appear in friends' lives and my life

These topics include construal, emotional misattribution, sense of self, confirmation bias, altruism, culture and thought, the self and family dynamics, social norms, compliance, and effort justification. I wrote each blog post on one or two of the topics, and hopefully the example I gave could explain what they mean.

Footnotes throughout the blog posts include what examples the scenario is from as well as what that topic of psychology means.

This website was created using a template from WowThemes.com. The website code as well as style has been modified by Rachel Zhou.
